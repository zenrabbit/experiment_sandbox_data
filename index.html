Designcraft for experiments open data

A set of remote exercises exploring experimental design for the life sciences. A total of three independent labs were done to focus on different design principles, sampling techniques, and taxa. Exercises are described in full in this open access manual entitled Designcraft for experiments. Data were collected by students in a third-year biology course offering at York University in Toronto, ON, Canada. Students collected the data and published individual datasets to figshare with a CCBY4.0 licensing. Data were then compiled for each lab including balcony birdwatching, backyard bioblitz, and solo surveys by M. Zuliani (ORCID: 0000-0001-8114-3609) and M. Owen (ORCID: 0000-0002-9931-4522). The provenance of each independent observation in the compiled data was recognized by attribution to the initials of the primary data author including citation via DOI and link to figshare.

Metadata
Record-level
year is the calendar year of work not term
experiment describes the three experimental options in designcraft book
source is either full experiment or pilot experiment
rep is the replicate per experimental set
location is the site name locally
habitat is the ecological classification
date is the calendar date for each observation

Attribute-level
bioblitz
taxa is a classification by vertebrate or invertebrate
species in the genus and species for observation

birds
frequency is the number observed within a 3-5min observation interval
behaviours are described in ethogram

solo-survey
vegetation surveys and species_richness is the total number of unique species observed per 0.5 x 0.5m quadrat
total cover is the total cover of vegetation within the 0.5m quadrat capped at 100
